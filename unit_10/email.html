creative director

Giota is an award-winning creative director of both Country Style and Vogue Living magazines with a demonstrated history of working in the publishing industry. Skilled in Magazine Design, Art Direction, Graphics, Typography and commissioning as well as Creating Content. She is a well respected, media and communication professional with a Bachelor of Design in Visual Communications from UTS Sydney.
